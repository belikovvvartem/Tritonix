TRITONIX STUDIO Privacy Policy

TRITONIX STUDIO places great importance on ensuring the privacy and protection of users' personal data. We strive to ensure every client understands how and why their data is used and feels confident in its security.

1. Collection and Use of Personal Data

TRITONIX STUDIO collects and processes personal data provided voluntarily by users through the order form or during communication with studio representatives. This data includes:

  • Client's first and last name for personalized communication;
  • Contact details (phone number, email) for prompt communication;
  • Details about the requested service (description, attached files).

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Processing and fulfilling client orders;
  • Providing feedback and consultations;
  • Improving our services, including analyzing client needs.

2. User Rights

Every user has the right to:

  • Access their personal data;
  • Correct their data;
  • Delete their data;
  • Restrict data processing;
  • Withdraw consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

TRITONIX STUDIO does not share your personal data with third parties unless it is necessary to fulfill obligations to the client. Such cases include:

  • Payment processing through payment systems;
  • Engaging courier or logistics services for delivering the final product.

4. Data Retention

Your personal data is stored on secure servers for the duration necessary to fulfill your order. The maximum retention period is three years after the order is completed, unless otherwise required by Ukrainian law.

5. Data Protection

TRITONIX STUDIO implements modern technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of personal data. We protect data from:

  • Unauthorized access or disclosure;
  • Accidental or intentional destruction or loss;
  • Unauthorized modification or misuse.

6. Cookies and Analytics

To enhance the user experience, our website may use cookies and analytics tools. This data does not include personal information and is used solely to optimize website performance.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: support@tritonixstudio.com

We are always ready to assist you and provide complete information about the processing of your personal data.
